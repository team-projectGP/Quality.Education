What is quality education?

📚

Beyond the classroom

Quality education means safe, stimulating environments, well-trained teachers, and inclusive access — regardless of gender, disability, or background.

Key targets (SDG 4.1 – 4.7)

4.1

Primary & secondary

All girls and boys complete free, equitable, and quality primary and secondary education.

4.4

Skills for work

Increase number of youth/adults with relevant skills (including technical/vocational) for employment and entrepreneurship.

4.5

Eliminate disparities

Equal access for vulnerable people, including persons with disabilities, indigenous peoples, and children in conflict zones.

4.7

Global citizenship

Education for sustainable development, human rights, gender equality, and peace.

Why education transforms lives

🧠

Cognitive & economic ripple

Each year of schooling raises earnings by 10% on average. Educated mothers are more than twice as likely to send their children to school.

  • ✅ 420 million people would be lifted from poverty if all adults completed secondary school.
  • ✅ Child marriage drops — girls with secondary education are 6x less likely to marry early.
  • ✅ More resilient communities, lower inequality.
